⚙️ How It Works
01

Preprocessing & CLAHE

Image is resized to 224×224, normalised with ImageNet statistics and contrast-enhanced via CLAHE.

02

ConvNeXt-Base Inference

ImageNet-22k pretrained backbone classifies the X-ray into NORMAL or PNEUMONIA.

03

Grad-CAM Visualisation

Gradient-weighted class activation maps highlight the regions that drove the prediction.

04

Risk Stratification

Risk score = 0.7 × P(Pneumonia) + 0.3 × CAM intensity → LOW / MODERATE / HIGH / CRITICAL.
