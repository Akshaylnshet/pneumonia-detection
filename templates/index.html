📅 Training Pipeline
Phase 1 — Warm-up (5 epochs)
Backbone frozen. Head trained with LinearLR warmup from 0.1×. Allows the new classification head to converge before backbone fine-tuning.
Phase 2 — Fine-tuning
Full network unfrozen with differential LRs. Cosine annealing schedule. Mixup (α=0.4, 50% batches) applied for regularisation.
Augmentation & Regularisation
RandomHorizontalFlip, RandomRotation (±15°), ColorJitter, CLAHE, Label Smoothing (0.1), class-weighted loss, AMP (FP16).
DDPM Synthetic Augmentation
A compact U-Net DDPM trained on 64×64 grayscale X-rays (cosine schedule, 1000 steps) generates synthetic samples to augment training data.
Evaluation & Checkpointing
Early stopping on validation AUC (patience 8). Best checkpoint saved at highest AUC. Evaluated on held-out test set with confusion matrix, ROC, and per-class report.
